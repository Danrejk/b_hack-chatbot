# Emergency Backpack ("Go-Bag") Checklist

General checklist for an emergency backpack to grab during a war, air-strike,
nuclear/radiation, or evacuation scenario. Adapt to the situation: fewer items
for short-term sheltering, the full list for evacuation.

## Core items

- Water (at least 1.5-2 liters per person) and a refillable bottle
- Non-perishable food for 2-3 days (energy bars, canned goods, a manual can
  opener)
- Copies of ID, passport, insurance, and medical documents (physical, plus a
  photo saved offline)
- Cash in small bills (card networks may not work)
- Prescription medication, a basic first aid kit, spare glasses if needed
- Flashlight or headlamp with spare batteries, plus a battery-powered or
  hand-crank radio
- Power bank and phone charger/cable
- Warm clothing, sturdy shoes, a blanket or emergency thermal blanket
- Whistle (to signal for help), multitool, duct tape

## Additional items for nuclear/radiation or chemical threats

- Dust masks (N95 or better)
- Plastic sheeting and tape to seal windows/doors
- A change of clothes to remove potentially contaminated outer layers

## Dependents

- Infant supplies (formula, diapers) if traveling with a baby
- Pet food and a leash/carrier if traveling with a pet
- Extra medication or mobility aids for anyone with additional needs
